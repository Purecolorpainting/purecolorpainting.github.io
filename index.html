About Pure Color Painting

Since 2009, Pure Color Painting has been the trusted painting contractor for homeowners and businesses throughout Macomb County, Michigan. Based in Chesterfield, we serve the entire Metro Detroit area with a commitment to quality craftsmanship and 100% customer satisfaction.

Every project we undertake is approached with meticulous attention to detail. We take pride in proper surface preparation and protecting your furnishings, ensuring a flawless finish that lasts for years to come.
